Temporal Seasonality (month): Identifies long-term cycles (e.g., summer vacations or winter holidays) that influence flight demand and weather-related disruptions.
Weekly Periodicity (day_of_week): Captures the behavioral shift between business travel (mid-week) and leisure travel (weekends).
Diurnal Cycles (hour): Represents daily operations, pinpointing peak congestion times and airport curfew effects.
Spatial Connectivity (route): Acts as a nominal identifier for a specific corridor, capturing unique constraints like distance, airspace traffic, and specific "Origin-Destination" dynamics.
